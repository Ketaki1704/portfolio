OCR based Newpaper App

OCR software attempts to replicate the combined functions of the human eye and brain, which is why it is referred to as artificial intelligence software. When the user will use this app, the app will recognize the patterns of the user's interest simultaneously using Artificial Intelligence. So, whenever the user will open this app the information or news related to the user's interest will be pointed up by the green box. If the news is not that interesting, then the box will become red.​ Hence it will become much easy to the user to read only the interested news he/she wants.​

Guest Management System

Skin Cancer Portal

The aim of our study is to trace down the class of skin cancer at an early stage using artificial intelligence thereby reducing the risk of death and increasing the chances of recovery of skin cancer patients. To achieve this we planned to build a one-stop portal where doctors can upload images of patients' skin lesions and understand the class of the skin cancer patient affected and provide the necessary remedies immediately.
